Open Source Guitar Pedal as MuzHack Workshop Topic

We wish to design an open source guitar effect pedal and give MuzHack workshops on building it

How Will We Do This

  • We will pay a Canadian PCB designer $120 for a custom circuit design
  • We will have the PCBs made cheaply in Germany
  • We will buy a batch of the required electronics components
  • We will enlist a local pedal maker to design and build enclosures for us
  • We will host the workshops at Betahaus
  • We will use the Betahaus distribution network to attract an audience
